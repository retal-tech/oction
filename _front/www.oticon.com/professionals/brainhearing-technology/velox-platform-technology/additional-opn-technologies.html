Additional Opn™ Technologies

Technologies

Opn™ builds on Oticon’s proven BrainHearing™ technologies with updates to several of our core features. 

The speed and power of the Velox™ platform enhances the performance of these features to help patients perform at their best in even the most challenging environments. In addition to Opn Sound Navigator™, Opn™ introduces new features and technologies designed to improve your patient’s overall listening experience. 

YouMatic™ LX
 

Tailors OpenSound Navigator™ to individual needs and preferences
 YouMatic™ LX is the personalization feature in Oticon Opn™ that intelligently controls the level of performance and response of the OpenSound Navigator™ across listening environments.

Speech Guard™ LX

 Improves speech understanding in noisy environments
Speech Guard LX preserves clear, transparent sound quality and speech details for better speech understanding with less effort even in complex environments.

Feedback shield LX

Dual-microphone feedback system eliminates feedback rapidly and effectively
Feedback is uncomfortable and annoying. With Feedback shield LX, Oticon Opn™ delivers ultra-fast and effective feedback management without compromising audibility or sound quality.

Clear Dynamics

Better sound quality in the full dynamic range of life
Clear Dynamics expands the input dynamic range, processing input sounds up to 113 dB SPL, to provide better sound quality without distortion and artifact at loud input levels, while still keeping the sound quality of soft input levels intact.

Soft Speech Booster LX

Improves soft speech understanding up to 20%
 Soft Speech Booster LX makes soft sounds audible to people with hearing loss. By increasing access to the soft sounds that occur in most situations and conversations, Soft Speech Booster LX improves soft speech understanding by up to 20%.

Wind Noise Management

Better access to speech in situations with wind noise
With the powerful Velox™ platform, Wind Noise Management offers innovative and highly efficient wind noise suppression. High speed estimators analyze the presence of wind noise 500 times per second in 16 frequency channels for fast and precise application of up to 30 dB wind noise reduction.
