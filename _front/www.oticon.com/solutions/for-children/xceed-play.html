Oticon Xceed Play

For children with severe-to-profound hearing loss

 

 

The world’s most powerful pediatric hearing aid

The revolutionary Oticon Xceed Play is the only hearing aid on the market that gives an open sound experience to children of all ages with severe-to-profound hearing loss.

Oticon Xceed Play gives your child:

 

  • BrainHearing™ technology

    It is essential that your child’s hearing aid supports the way the brain naturally makes sense of sound. Oticon Xceed Play provides your child’s brain with the best possible conditions to hear in a way that helps their brain learn and develop.

  • 360° access to speech

    Children with severe-to-profound hearing loss need more support to separate voices from noise. Oticon Xceed Play gives your child access to clearer speech from all directions for better listening and learning.

  • Prevention of whistling sounds

    Groundbreaking technology in Oticon Xceed Play helps prevent feedback from happening so that your child can enjoy a clearer, stable speech signal*, and receive up to 20% more speech details,** which are essential to language development.

  • Easy wireless connectivity

    Children and teenagers with severe-to-profound hearing loss greatly appreciate accessories and add-ons that make their everyday lives easier by improving the sound signal to their hearing aids.

    Read More

 

*Ng 2019, Oticon Whitepaper
**For prescribed fittings, according to best practice

 

 

Xceed Play hearing aids

A breakthrough in hearing care for children with severe-to-profound hearing loss

Oticon Xceed Play comes in two styles: BTE super power (SP) and BTE ultra power (UP) each suited for severe-to-profound hearing loss. With 12 different colors to pick from, your child can choose to stand out with bright and colorful options or select colors that compliment hair and skin tones. Both styles also include a fun collection of decorative stickers your child can use to personalize their hearing aids.

Explore the styles and colors of Xceed Play

 

 

Help your child connect easily with the world around them

Oticon Xceed Play connects wirelessly to modern smartphones and gives your child access to a wide range of accessories that make it easier to take part in school, enjoy music and have fun with friends.

 

  • ConnectClip

    Oticon’s ConnectClip remote microphone makes it easier for your child to communicate with friends and family where distance or noise is involved.

    Explore ConnectClip
  • Classroom solutions

    Xceed Play offers a broad range of classroom connectivity options to support learning, including providing clear access to audio sound and a teacher’s voice using Oticon EduMic.

    Explore Classroom Solutions
  • Oticon ON App

    The app offers a wide range of features making it easy to control and use Xceed Play hearing aids.

    Learn about the ON App
