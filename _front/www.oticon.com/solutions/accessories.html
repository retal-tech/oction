Hearing aid accessories

for life's many opportunities

 




Better hearing starts with choosing a hearing solution that’s adapted to you and gives your brain what it needs to make sense of sound. Explore the range of practical hearing aid accessories that enhance your everyday activities and add convenience to your life. Oticon offers everything from wireless hearing aid accessories, to child-friendly and classroom solutions, and much more.




Rechargeable hearing aids

A full day’s power, every day.

Oticon offers discreet, rechargeable hearing aids, including Oticon More™, Play PX and Zircon, with easy-to-use chargers. Add the sleek portable SmartCharger model with a durable, protective case to the desktop model that comes with your hearing aids for reliable recharging wherever life takes you. More power. More freedom. More flexibility.

Now available: hands-free conversations on your home phone

No more running for the phone. No missed calls. No feedback worries.

 

The updated Phone Adapter 2.0 now pairs with ConnectClip and works with your existing home analog landline phone to turn your hearing instruments into a wireless headset. During a call, your hearing aids become the headset, and the ConnectClip, attached near your collar, functions as a microphone. To answer a call, simply press a button on your ConnectClip. The Phone Adapter 2.0 plus ConnectClip support both incoming and outgoing phone calls.

Additional Oticon Hearing Aid Accessories

 

  • Oticon Classroom Solutions

    Oticon offers a range of flexible classroom solutions that are designed to help children and teens overcome the effects of distance and noise and improve speech understanding. EduMic is a wireless remote microphone system that allows students to receive stable and clear access to their teacher’s voice along with the added benefits of our innovative OpenSound Navigator™ technology.

    Explore classroom solutions

  • Oticon SafeLine™

    Hearing aid retention cord for those high-adventure moments! With SafeLine, you can be confident that hearing aids wont be lost, fall off, or become damaged. SafeLine comes in two lengths and is compatible with all Oticon BTE and miniRITE hearing aid styles.

    SafeLine meets international legislation and standards for safety and all materials used in SafeLine have been chosen and thoroughly evaluated as fully biocompatible. Oticon SafeLine is available through your hearing care professional.

    Find a hearing care professional
