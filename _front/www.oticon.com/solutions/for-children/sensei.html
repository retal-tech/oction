Grow up with a rich sound picture

Children are busy growing and learning. They experience a huge range of sound environments every day, and their ability to understand speech wherever they are is vital to their development and confidence. Unique to Oticon’s speech processing features inside, Sensei preserves the fine details of sound that help your child perceive speech clearly, naturally and distinctly.

Sensei is designed for child development and adapts to provide optimized hearing for any child with hearing loss, in whatever environments they participate in — school, sport, hobbies or play. The array of advanced, automatic and coordinated systems — such as complex signal processing, adaptive directionality, noise management and feedback cancellation systems — give your child speech intelligibility in any listening situation. Thanks to BrainHearing™ technologies, your child is able to develop their auditory systems as naturally as possible, minimizing their cognitive load. Oticon Sensei offers you peace of mind at every step of your child’s journey to adulthood.

Sensei offers a better future for every child with hearing loss

Made for childhood

Children with hearing loss are just as full of life and energy as any other children. Sensei incorporates many features to ensure robust and safe hearing aids to fit their many changing needs as they grow from infancy to adolescence. The hearing aids undergo real-life testing to ensure their strength and resilience.

Keep up with confidence

Hearing the voices of teachers in noisy classrooms and lecture halls can be a challenge for children and teenagers with hearing loss. Amigo FM is designed to help them overcome the effects of distance and noise, so they can pick up as much learning as possible.

Matching the colors of childhood

The Sensei family consists of a variety of cool colors and stickers to make your child’s hearing aid their own.

SafeLine retention cord

Extra safety for active children​

Peace of mind for parents

Oticon’s SafeLine retention cord lets kids wear their hearing aids while playing or running around without compromising on safety. Simply clip on and play – no more worries about hearing aids falling off and getting lost.

The unique design features a breakaway cord with a quick-release clasp that will easily open if it gets snagged or pulled. It is also hypoallergenic and perfect for children in all aspects of safe use.

SafeLine is available in two lengths to ensure safety for kids of all ages. SafeLine meets international legislation and standards for safety and biocompatibility.

  • Prevents hearing aids from falling off, getting lost or getting stepped on

  • Only retention solution on the market with unique breakaway cord

  • Two lengths (17” and 22”) to meet safety requirements for babies and toddlers, as well as serve the needs of older children involved in sports and other adventures

  • One SafeLine device will automatically accompany every Sensei, Sensei SP or other Oticon instrument order for children ages 0-6 years

  • SafeLine also available upon request for children over 6 years at no charge with a hearing aid order
