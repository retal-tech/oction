Demand the best products

Our family of premium products takes the open sound experience to the next level by enabling speech understanding in noisy environments with less listening effort.*

*Compared to Oticon Opn S™, Santurette et al. 2020. Oticon Whitepaper

  • Oticon More™

    NEW miniBTEs and miniRITEs

    Taking our revolutionary BrainHearing™ technologies to the next level, we created a hearing aid that works more like how the brain works, because it learned through experience. Oticon More is the world’s first hearing aid that uses a fully trained, on-board Deep Neural Network (DNN) to process the sound scene in a more precise and balanced way.

    Read more

  • Oticon Opn™ Custom

    In-the-ear

    Oticon has integrated the high-quality components behind our open sound technology into a shell that's smaller than ever. Now, more patients can be fit with the small custom hearing aids they desire without compromising quality, reliability or performance.

Pediatrics

Our hearing devices for children and teens help them grow, thrive, and learn by improving speech understanding to help them meet their developmental needs.

  • Oticon Play PX

    miniRITEs and miniBTEs

    New Oticon Play PX is the world’s first pediatric hearing aid that learned the way children learn — through experience thanks to its on-board Deep Neural Network (DNN). The DNN was trained on 12 million real-life sound scenes and learned to recognize sounds through experience, like children’s brains do naturally.

    Read more

  • Oticon Xceed Play

    Super and Ultra power

    This revolutionary hearing aid is the only one on the market with an open sound experience for children with severe to profound hearing loss. BrainHearing technology allows children to make sense of the sounds around them as they learn and develop.

    Read more


Power users

The world’s most powerful hearing aid gives your patients access to more speech than ever before to help reduce the listening effort this patient population struggles with each day.

  • Oticon Xceed

    Super and Ultra power

    Xceed provides patients with severe-to-profound hearing loss with the world’s most powerful hearing aid. Static and whistling are now a thing of the past with OpenSound Optimizer™ technology which provides a stable speech signal throughout the day.

    Read more


Single-sided deafness

The world’s first CROS/BiCROS solution that supports speech awareness and wireless streaming at the same time, providing a revolutionary sound experience for patients with single-sided deafness.

  • Oticon CROS PX and Oticon CROS

    miniRITE R and miniRITE T

    The Oticon CROS family is the world’s first CROS/BiCROS hearing aids with advanced technologies proven to support speech awareness in challenging listening environments and wireless streaming at the same time.
