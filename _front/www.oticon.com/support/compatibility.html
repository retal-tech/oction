Introducing Hands-free Communication

With an iPhone or iPad you can now use your Oticon More, Zircon or Play PX hearing aids* to make or receive hand-free phone or video calls.

Hands-free communication is available with iPhone 11 or later, iPad Pro 12.9-inch (5th generation and 4th generation), iPad Pro 11-inch (3rd generation and 2nd generation), iPad Air (4th generation), and iPad mini (6th generation), with software updates iOS 15.2 and iPadOS 15.2 or later.

Now enjoy the simple convenience of hands-free calls whenever it suits you. With your hands free, you can talk to your family, friends and colleagues when you’re on the go.

* Oticon More hearing aids require firmware 1.3.0 to enable hands-free communication functionality.



One-way direct streaming from Android devices

Oticon More, Zircon and Play PX hearing aids are compatible with the new Android accessibility protocol, Audio Streaming for Hearing Aids (ASHA) allowing high-quality, one-way direct streaming from Android devices with Android 10 or later and Bluetooth 5.0. Please note that this list may change as Android operating systems are updated.

Apple® devices: iOS 14.0 or later.
Android™ devices: Android OS 8.0 or later.

Oticon ON app

Features in Oticon ON app:

• Remote control
• HearingFitness
• Tinnitus SoundSupport
• IFTTT

System and software requirements:

Apple devices: iOS 14.0 or later.
Android devices: Android OS Version 8.0 (Oreo) or later.

Compatible hearing aids:

Oticon More hearing aids: Firmware 1.0.0 or later.
Oticon Zircon hearing aids: Firmware 1.1. or later
Oticon Play PX hearing aids: Firmware 1.1 or later
Oticon Opn hearing aids: Firmware 6.0 or later to run HearingFitness.
Oticon Siya hearing aids: Firmware 1.0 or later.
Oticon Ruby hearing aids: Firmware 1.0 or later.
Oticon Opn S hearing aids: Firmware 7.0 or later to run HearingFitness.
Oticon Opn Play hearing aids: Firmware 7.0 to run HearingFitness.
Oticon Xceed hearing aids: Firmware 8.0 or later.
Oticon Xceed Play hearing aids: Firmware 8.0 or later.

Tip for iPad users:

To download the Oticon ON app on iPad, search for Oticon, and use the filter: iPhone only.

Info for Apple Watch users:

Apple Watch supports volume and program changes.

 

Compatible Oticon wireless accessories

ConnectClip

ConnectClip works directly with your Oticon hearing aids and can also be used with the Oticon ON app.


Mobile device and accessory requirement:

Bluetooth 2.1 +EDR or later.


Other Oticon accessories:


• Remote control 3.0
• TV Adapter 3.0
• Phone Adapter 2.0 (requires ConnectClip)
• BTD 800 USB dongle (requires ConnectClip)
