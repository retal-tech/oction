Oticon Opn sound quality
available in custom styles

Through progressive design improvements, we integrated the high-quality components behind our open sound technology into a shell that’s smaller than ever. Now, more patients can be fit with the small custom hearing aids they desire without compromising quality, reliability or performance.

 

Oticon Opn Custom Highlights:


  • Oticon Opn sound quality

  • Open SoundNavigator™ — available in all styles

     

    • Now including a new optimized design for one microphone instruments

  • Twinlink™ wireless communication with 2.4 GHz wireless technology*

  • Our smallest IIC ever

  • Five faceplate color options

  • Optional push button and volume wheel

  • Larger battery options for extended battery life and ease of handling

  • Telecoil to help with phone/loop systems and easy phone access

 

All custom styles in the Oticon Opn family are powered by the Velox™ platform and deliver the unique open sound experience.
This comprehensive range of custom hearing aids covers hearing
loss from mild to severe, ensuring a perfect Opn fit for almost any
audiological need.

Oticon Opn
Available in three performance levels

Each Opn style is available in three performance levels with increasing degrees of BrainHearing™ support. The advanced technologies in Opn work in combination to help the brain make sense of sound in everyday listening environments even when there are multiple speakers.

The three levels differ in the amount of support they give the brain in terms of rapid noise reduction, localization of sounds, speech clarity, and the personalization of the listening experience.

Opn Technology

Opn is powered by the Velox™ platform, offering our best in resolution and speed. This powerful platform makes it possible for Opn to host specialized technologies that open the world to more users with a wide range of needs, as well as offering streamer-free connectivity to smartphones, audio devices, and to other smart devices through the Internet.

 

TwinLink™ technology — a world's first

Oticon Opn features two dedicated communication systems — one for outstanding binaural processing and one for powerful direct streaming to external devices.

Learn about TwinLink™ technology

If This Then That (IFTTT) — connect to the internet of things

Oticon Opn is the world’s first hearing aid that connects to the Internet via the If This Then That network (IFTTT.com). Explore the limitless possibilities available for your patients and help them craft their own connections to a range of IFTTT-enabled devices used in everyday life.

Craft your own connections

The key to an open sound experience

The OpenSound Navigator is the most powerful processing technology in Oticon Opn. It works so fast and precisely that it can attenuate unwanted noise – even between words in an open soundscape. In this way, Oticon Opn makes it easier for the brain to decide who and what to listen to, and to switch attention when needed.

Learn more about OpenSound Navigator

Tinnitus SoundSupport™

This proven sound generator technology is integrated into Oticon Opn hearing aids. Select from a wide range of cusomizable tinnitus and relief sounds that are dynamic, yet soothing, including broadband and natural, ocean-like sounds.

Learn about Tinnitus SoundSupport technology

 

Help patients stay connected with ConnectClip

ConnectClip turns Opn hearing aids into a high quality, wireless headset for hands-free calls and for listening to music or podcasts in stereo. It works with any modern smartphone, music player or computer. ConnectClip also functions as a wireless, remote microphone, as well as a remote control for Opn hearing aids.

Learn more about ConnectClip
