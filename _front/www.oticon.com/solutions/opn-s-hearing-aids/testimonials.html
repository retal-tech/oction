Hear what users have to say about Oticon Opn S™

 

Opn S gives you a 360° sound experience

Oticon Opn S users are enjoying an improved listening experience thanks to BrainHearing™ technology. This technology helps the brain and ears work together, providing better speech understanding and reducing listening effort. Studies prove Oticon Opn S users experience speech understanding at the same level as people with normal hearing.*

*Juul Jensen 2018, Oticon Whitepaper

In emergency situations where communication is critical, Clare relies on her Opn S hearing aids to help her tune out background noise and focus on what's important. With Opn S, she can hear the instructions of her crew mates or commands on her radio during noisy fire rescues, when every minute counts.

"My Opn S hearing aids have made me a better firefighter." – Clare W.
