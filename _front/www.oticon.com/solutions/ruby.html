Oticon Ruby

Made to move

Made to capture special moments

Oticon Ruby is a line of behind-the-ear hearing aids designed to give you the confidence you need to live life to the fullest. Ruby improves your speech understanding so you can experience the joys of life. It’s time to get out and hear what you’ve been missing. Ruby is made to move.


Oticon Ruby highlights:


  • Superb sound quality

  • Easy connectivity

  • Rechargeable solution

  • Choice of styles and colors

Meet the Ruby lineup



Which style is right for you?

Oticon Ruby comes in a choice of five behind-the-ear styles and colors designed to match your lifestyle.


See all styles & colors


Talk to your hearing care professional to discuss the best option for you.

Made for a full day’s power. Every day.

Ruby miniRITE R offers the simplicity of rechargeable batteries. This discreet hearing aid comes with a sleek and stylish charger and long-lasting rechargeable batteries.

Learn about rechargeables

Made to make life easier

Ruby’s wireless connectivity delivers high quality sound streaming so you can connect to the devices and people that mean the most. Ruby allows you to watch videos, stream music, take phone calls and much more—right from your hearing aids. Ruby connects to Oticon’s extensive range of supporting apps, remote controls and multiple TV adapters so you have the freedom to move seamlessly throughout the day.
