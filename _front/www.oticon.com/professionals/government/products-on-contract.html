Products on the VA Hearing Aid Contract

Oticon offers a wide range of hearing solutions to our nation’s Veterans and active duty military personnel

 

More potential than ever

In a wide range of innovative styles


Choosing Oticon More™ has never been easier. There are so many possibilities! An entire family of hearing aids including two new miniBTEs now stands ready to meet your patients’ diverse needs and preferences.

New Styles Available May 1

Building on our commitment to BrainHearing™, we designed Oticon More to give the brain more of the important information it needs to make sense of sounds. We developed a new approach to sound processing that incorporated an on-board Deep Neural Network (DNN) that works more like how the brain works because it learned through experience.

In addition, the scientific proof that continues to build, with new evidence that confirms Oticon More reduces sustained listening effort by an astounding 30%.* This new evidence speaks for itself, “We give the brain what it needs to hear.”

Highlights

  • Clinical evidence shows that Oticon More helps the brain work properly.

  • NEW miniBTE styles join the existing miniRITE family to bring the superior sound quality of Oticon More to more patients.

  • Now your Oticon More patients can have seamless two-way hands-free phone or video calls with select iPhones and iPads, as well as direct streaming to select Android™ devices.**

Explore Oticon More    Request demo instruments

*Murmu Nielsen, R. & Ng, E. 2021. Reducing sustained listening effort: Oticon More new evidence. Oticon whitepaper.
**Android devices need to support ASHA to allow direct connectivity to Oticon More. iPhone and iPad devices need to support two-way hands-free communication. Please visit oticon.com/support/compatibility for more information and to see which devices are compatible.
