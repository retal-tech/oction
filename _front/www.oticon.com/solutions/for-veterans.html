Hearing aids for Veterans

If you’re diagnosed with hearing loss, your VHA benefits include Oticon products. We have a full line of instruments designed to address the entire range of hearing needs. Ask for Oticon by name.

 

Oticon More™ is Expanding

Available in VA audiology clinics from coast to coast on May 1, 2022

With clear and superior sound quality, new BTE styles and charging options, you’ll get more out of life with Oticon More.

 


Features and benefits of Oticon More hearing aids



  • Supports your brain

    Oticon More gives your brain more of what it needs to make better sense of sound

  • An on-board Deep Neural Network (DNN)

    The DNN in Oticon More is trained with 12 million sounds from real life to recognize virtually all types of sounds to support your brain

  • Better speech understanding with less effort

    Oticon More is proven to provide superior sound. It delivers 30% more sound to the brain,* provides yet another 15% increase in speech understanding* and reduces listening effort by an astounding 30% over time**

  • A full day’s power

    Oticon More miniRITE R and miniBTE R rechargeable styles take just 3 hours*** to charge for a full day’s power†

  • Effortless connection & two-way hands-free communication

    Enjoy the convenience of two‑way hands‑free communication with select iPhone and iPad devices and direct streaming from select Android™ devices††

Xceed

People with severe-to-profound hearing loss rely on hearing aids to get through each and every day, and it can be extremely challenging to follow the conversation. With the new groundbreaking technology in Oticon Xceed, you will now have the support you need to actively take part in these situations without having to rely on guessing and lip reading.

Charge into life with more power, and flexibility

The Oticon More miniRITE R and new mininBTE R are discreet lithium-ion based rechargeable styles that provides a full day of battery life,* including streaming. Oticon More rechargeable hearing aids give you better hearing and speech understanding in a variety of environments, with less effort that gives convenient, worry-free rechargeability each day. New easy to use portable SmartCharger gives you more flexibility to enjoy life on the go while keeping Oticon More powered up, dry and protected.

*Lithium-ion performance varies depending on hearing loss, lifestyle and streaming behavior.
