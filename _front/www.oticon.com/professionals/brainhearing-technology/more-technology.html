MoreSound Intelligence™

A quantum leap in signal processing

MoreSound Intelligence is a groundbreaking technology that makes it easier for patients to enjoy, follow and engage in conversations. It comprehensively scans and precisely analyzes the sound scene. Then it organizes the sounds around the user and utilizes our highly trained, on‑board Deep Neural Network (DNN) to process and create contrast among the identified sounds.

The result is a more precise and natural representation of all sounds in a complete and balanced sound scene. This helps the brain orient and focus better, with clear information that makes it easier to make sense of sound for improved speech understanding.

Read MSI Tech Paper


MoreSound Amplifier™

Dynamic, balanced amplification system that seamlessly adapts to the sound scene

The MoreSound Amplifier is a trailblazing balanced amplification system. It seamlessly adapts its resolution and speed to the nature of the sound scene at hand. And it works precisely and quickly to ensure that important details and dynamics are made audible and available, while maintaining the fine contrast and balance among sounds.* This ensures the brain has access to the important information it needs to make sense of sound and can focus quickly on what is most important.**

  • Six-times increase in resolution for more precise amplification

  • Adaptive speed pilot makes the sound scene available while maintaining contrast and balance between sounds

  • Constant prioritizing of 4-channel or 24-channel paths based on incoming signal

MoreSound Optimizer™

An extremely fast feedback system that detects and prevents feedback proactively

MoreSound Optimizer pioneers the way feedback management systems works by using a patented technique called Spectro Temporal Modulation (STM). STM uses breaker-signals to manage feedback, eliminating the need for immediate gain reduction. In fact, fittings can have a positive loop gain of up to 6 dB, which allows for optimal gain and a more open fitting without the risk of audible feedback. The result is accessing speech details with more natural sound, increased comfort, and improved speech understanding — even in the most challenging listening environments.

The state-of-the-art rechargeable solution full of options

The Oticon More miniRITE R and miniBTE R are discreet lithium-ion based rechargeable styles that provide a full day of battery life,* including streaming, after just three hours of charging.** They come with a t-coil and are compatible with the standard desktop charger or the optional, portable SmartCharger that includes a built-in power bank and protective lid for convenient, reliable charging for patients on the go.

• Full day of battery life,* including streaming, after just three hours of charging**

• Quick recharge—30 minutes for an additional six hours of power***

*Lithium-ion performance varies depending on hearing loss, lifestyle and streaming behavior
**miniRITE R 3 hours, miniBTE R 3.5 hours
***30 minutes gives an additional six hours for miniRITE R, 30 minutes gives an additional five hours for miniBTE R
