Oticon provides hearing care professionals with solutions, tools, and techniques to help you offer every child with hearing loss a better future.

Oticon Play PX

Unleash the magic of childhood

New Oticon Play PX is the world’s first pediatric hearing aid technology that learned the way children learn — through experience, thanks to the world’s first on-board Deep Neural Network (DNN). The DNN was trained on 12 million real-life sound scenes and learned to recognize sounds through experience, like children’s brains do naturally.

Powered by Polaris™ — our most intelligent and powerful platform ever — Oticon Play PX is available in rechargeable and non-rechargeable styles and has been built to withstand children’s active lives.

With Oticon Play PX, children can enjoy two-way hands-free phone and video calls from select iPhones and iPads and direct streaming from select Android™ devices.* Oticon Play PX is also compatible with a wide range of connectivity options for enhanced learning and communication, with streaming from classroom electronics, TVs, computers, smartphones and more.

Learn more about Oticon Play PX
Download Product Guide


*Android devices need to support ASHA to allow direct streaming to Oticon Play PX. iPhone and iPad devices need to support two-way hands-free communication. Please visit oticon.com/support/compatibility for more information and to see which devices are compatible.

Oticon EduMic

Oticon EduMic is a wireless remote microphone system offering world class sound quality, ease of use, and multiple connectivity options. Designed specifically to overcome the everyday challenges of listening in the classroom, Oticon EduMic is compatible with Oticon’s pediatric hearing aids* as well as existing classroom systems.

  • World class sound quality with OpenSound Navigator™ technology

  • 2.4 GHz low energy Bluetooth® technology to provide long battery life and a stable signal

  • Ability to link Oticon hearing aids to existing remote microphone systems

  • Wind noise management system to help students during outdoor learning


*EduMic is compatible with all 2.4 GHz hearing aids based on the Velox™, Velox S™ and Polaris platforms.

Oticon Xceed Play

The world’s most powerful pediatric hearing aid

Oticon Xceed Play is the first pediatric power instrument with OpenSound Navigator™ and OpenSound Optimizer™, BrainHearing technologies that support 360° access to speech. Available in 2 performance levels, 2 styles (SP and UP), and 12 child-friendly colors, Oticon Xceed Play creates a life changing experience for children with severe-to-profound hearing loss by allowing them to connect with loved ones, grow, thrive, and learn.

Oticon EduMic is a vital partner to Oticon’s Xceed Play hearing aids. It delivers stable and clear access to the teacher’s voice in the classroom by continuously monitoring the environment and removing unwanted noise — even between words.

Oticon Opn Play™ — child-friendly hearing care

Offering 2 performance levels, 4 styles — including miniRITE R with rechargeable lithium-ion batteries — and 12 child-friendly colors, Oticon Opn Play helps children of all ages open up to the world. Powered by the Velox S™ platform, Oticon Opn Play unleashes the full potential of the open sound paradigm for children. With access to 360° sound from the environment, Opn Play helps children hear sounds and increases speech understanding in noise.

For school-age children, Oticon EduMic and Opn Play partner together to give direct access to the teacher’s voice, helping students overcome the challenges of the modern classroom.

Ear-level FM compatibility

Children need access to the very best speech, especially in learning environments, whether in the classroom or a dance class. Even the best hearing aids can’t overcome all of the listening challenges of distance or noise — in these situations FM is a reliable go-to technology.

Xceed Play and Opn Play BTE PP can be fit with ear-level FM:

  • Design-integrated Amigo R12G2 receiver in colors to match Xceed Play and Opn Play

  • Universal FM/DM options via the FM10 audio shoe

  • Requires use of FM adapter battery door (includes tamper-resistant option)


Oticon EduMic is compatible with integrated FM receivers and universal FM options.
