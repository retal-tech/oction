Hearing aids and accessories

Life-changing technology designed to empower people with hearing loss

All of our hearing solutions are built with our BrainHearing™ philosophy and have been designed to support your brain’s natural process of making sense of sound. We offer a complete range of products to support a variety of hearing needs.



Expanded Oticon solutions for everyone

At Oticon, we never stop pushing the boundaries of what’s possible. Building on our BrainHearing™ philosophy, all of our solutions are designed to provide access to the full sound scene, which allows the brain to work in a more natural way. We not only revolutionized hearing care with Oticon More™, but we’re also introducing new hearing aid families and styles designed to help you enjoy a life full of sound.




More potential
than ever

With Oticon More, you get superior sound quality, now with more styles and options. Choose what suits your lifestyle- a miniRITE T or NEW miniBTE T with disposable batteries or a miniRITE R or NEW miniBTE R rechargeable style for overnight charging. With more styles, features and charging options, you can get more out of life.
