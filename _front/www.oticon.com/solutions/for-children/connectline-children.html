ConnectLine

Empowering Children and Teens

Today, our virtual world offers endless choices for children to spend more and more time online, interacting and connecting.

Helping children & teens fit in

It’s important to be able to follow what is going on in class, to socialize with friends anywhere and to enjoy music, gaming and films on equal terms with others.

Mobile phones, MP3-players, computers and TV can put even the most sophisticated hearing instruments to the test. Signals from external audio sources are best received directly and wirelessly, and with ConnectLine children can connect to any device at the touch of a button and receive clear sound right into their hearing aids.

Power to connect. Freedom to explore

Streamer Pro – the heart of ConnectLine – puts your kids in total control of volume as well as of all sources of audio-based information, entertainment and communication.

Streamer Pro turns a pair of hearing aids into a small wireless headset by picking up the audio signal and sending it straight into both ears. With no detectable delays in transmission, audio signals perfectly match any visual images when watching TV or using a computer.

Streamer Pro has been developed with hearing aid users in mind so that daily use is straightforward and intuitive. Streamer Pro is compatible with all wireless Oticon hearing aids and ConnectLine devices for seamless, hassle-free upgrades.

Streamer Pro - the gateway to a world of communication

  • Long battery life

    The battery life allows up to eight hours of streaming time for a full day’s use.

  • Compact, user-friendly design

    Features texturized rubber edges for easier handling and a more comfortable grip.

  • Intuitive operation

    Three buttons make it possible to instantly select and activate the application needed.


Keep up with confidence

Students who depend on FM can expand the functionality of the ConnectLine Streamer to the classroom and lecture hall. A wide range of FM receivers can be connected to the Streamer Pro, streaming FM to both ears with only one receiver.

Connect an FM receiver directly to Streamer Pro and the wireless FM signal can be streamed to both hearing aids. The Streamer’s built-in socket offers compatibility with a wide range of universal FM receivers. We recommend using the Amigo FM receiver.

Use your smartphone as a remote

For the young, tech-savvy users, the ConnectLine App is ideal to use without having to touch either the instruments OR the Streamer Pro. 

The ConnectLine App turns the smartphone or tablet into a cleverly personalized, discreet and easy to use remote control for the individual ConnectLine elements. Swipe, see and select to access and customize features and functions of the ConnectLine system and get a clear overview.
