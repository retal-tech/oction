Office Management Software can improve your efficiencies and patient experience

Oticon’s strategic partner, Blueprint Solutions Office Management Software (OMS) provides a leading cloud-based audiology software for hearing health care providers throughout the world. It seamlessly integrates your website and your OMS schedule so patients can make their appointments online 24/7. Blueprint will automatically send appointment reminders via email or text so patients can confirm their appointments with a single click.

  • Reduce no–shows

  • Enhance your patient experience

Connect with more patients

YHN was created to support and promote hearing health and that starts with you — the provider.

 

  • Empower your practice with YHN and Managed Care

     

    Patients come to your practice to find solutions that enable them to hear better. YHN’s partnerships, experts and resources are here to help broaden your access to more patients within your area.

  • The need for care is growing!

     

    YHN is your advocate helping you reach and treat more people seeking hearing health solutions. Our savvy partnerships and experienced negotiators secure the highest quality health plans that direct new patients to your practice. Providers are automatically enrolled in all applicable plans and if you need credentialing, we’ll get you there.

 

Join YHN... it's free!


YHN’s managed care reimbursement opportunities include: Federal plans, unions, Medicaid, Medicare Advantage and supplement plans, Preferred Provider Organizations (PPO), Health Management Organizations (HMO), workers’ compensation, vocational rehabilitation, and discount plans through associations, affiliations, and senior care organizations.

 

As an Oticon customer and a Your Hearing Network affiliate you may be eligible for additional support.
