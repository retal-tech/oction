Rechargeable hearing aids

Charge into life with more power, freedom and flexibility

Superb sound quality and groundbreaking features, all in one compact device. Oticon More™, new Oticon Zircon and new Oticon Play PX miniRITE R and new miniBTE R rechargeable styles provide a full day of battery life* after just three hours of charging.** The miniRITE R and miniBTE R are also compatible with the optional, portable SmartCharger*** or the standard Desktop Charger for enhanced charging flexibility.

We also offer the great benefits of miniRITE R rechargeable hearing aids in the Oticon Opn S™ family for adults and Oticon Opn Play™ family for children

 

*Lithium-ion performance varies depending on hearing loss, lifestyle and streaming behavior.
**Three and a half hours gives a full day for miniBTE R.
***Check with your hearing care professional about availability for the miniBTE R SmartCharger.

It couldn't be easier to use Oticon rechargeable hearing aids. Simply set your hearing aids in their desktop charger at night, and you'll wake up to fully-powered devices in the morning. A new, easy-to-use portable SmartCharger gives you more freedom to enjoy life on the go.

Our lithium-ion battery technology ensures you can comfortably enjoy a full day of hearing,* including streaming from your favorite devices.

The state-of-the-art rechargeable solution

Oticon More miniBTE R and miniRITE R come with a stylish desktop charger that takes just three hours* to give you a full day of battery life† — including if you stream sound from other devices.

• No disposable batteries means less hassle and less waste
• Just three hours to give you a full day of battery life† – including if you stream sound from other devices
• Quick recharge of 30 minutes gives an additional six hours of power**
• Convenient, easy-to-use, and reliable

*Three and a half hours gives a full day for miniBTE R.
**30 minutes give an additional five hours for miniBTE R.
†Lithium-ion battery performance varies depending on hearing loss, lifestyle and streaming behavior.

 

Oticon's easy-to-use hearing aid charger

 

  • Connect the charger to a power source using the integrated cable.

  • Easy to insert with one hand – no lid to open, and no need to turn off your hearing aids.

  • Place hearing aids in the charging ports to automatically begin charging via contact-free inductive technology.

  • Discreet LED lights let you see charging status at a glance.

The reliable Charger that goes with you

Oticon offers discreet hearing aids with easy-to-use chargers.
Add the sleek portable SmartCharger* with a durable, protective case to the desktop charger that comes with your Oticon More, Oticon Zircon or Oticon Play PX hearing aids for reliable recharging wherever life takes you.

• Charges hearing aids in three hours** for a full day of power† including streaming
• 30-minute quick charge provides an additional six hours of power***
• Designed for travel with a protective lid and built-in power bank
• Drying function automatically removes any moisture build-up from hearing aids to keep them working and ready for use

*Check with your hearing care professional about availability for the miniBTE R SmartCharger.
**Three and a half hours gives a full day for miniBTE R.
***30 minutes gives an additional five hours for miniBTE R.
†Lithium-ion battery performance varies depending on hearing loss, lifestyle and streaming behavior.
