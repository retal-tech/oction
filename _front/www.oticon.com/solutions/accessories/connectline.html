ConnectLine

ConnectLine links Alta2, Nera2, Ria2, Dynamo and Sensei hearing aids wirelessly to cell phones, tablets, TVs and other Bluetooth audio devices. ConnectLine makes it easy and convenient to use your hearing instruments as a personal wireless headset for richer, more enjoyable sound.

Connect to devices

ConnectLine will help enhance your communication challenges across the board. You'll find it easier and more satisfying to:

  • Talk on the phone

  • Watch TV

  • Listen to music

  • Video chat, and more

Learn about using ConnectLine

Streamer Pro

Your gateway to connectivity

Streamer Pro puts you in total control, not only of program shifts and hearing aid volume, but of all sources of audio-based information, entertainment and communication.

By picking up the audio signal and sending it straight into both ears, the Streamer turns a pair of hearing aids into a small wireless headset. With no detectable delays in transmission, your audio signal and the speaker’s lips match perfectly. This can make all the difference when watching TV or using a computer.

A fully integrated system, Streamer Pro has been developed and thoroughly tested with hearing aid users in mind so that daily use is straightforward and intuitive. 

Looking much like an MP3 player, the Streamer is worn around the neck and features three dedicated buttons for the core applications: phone, TV and microphone, and also functions as a remote control. Streamer Pro is compatible with all Alta2, Nera2, Ria2, Dynamo and Sensei solutions.

  • Long battery life

    The battery life allows up to eight hours of streaming time for a full day’s use.

  • Compact, user-friendly design

    Features texturized rubber edges for easier handling and a more comfortable grip.

  • Intuitive operation

    Three buttons make it possible to instantly select and activate the application needed.

Keep up with confidence

Students who depend on FM can expand the functionality of the ConnectLine Streamer to the classroom and lecture hall. A wide range of FM receivers can be connected to the ConnectLine Streamer Pro, streaming FM to both ears with only one receiver.

Connect an FM receiver directly to Streamer Pro and the wireless FM signal can be streamed to both hearing aids. The Streamer’s built-in socket offers compatibility with a wide range of universal FM receivers. We recommend using the Amigo FM receiver.
